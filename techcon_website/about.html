Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago with the vision to connect technology enthusiasts, professionals, and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated events in the tech industry.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we aim to foster innovation, knowledge-sharing, and collaboration across all areas of technology. We believe in empowering individuals to create a better future through technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed many influential voices in tech:

  • Ada Lovelace: Visionary of computing and early innovator in algorithmic thinking.
  • Alan Turing: Pioneer of computer science and artificial intelligence.
  • Grace Hopper: Inventor of the first compiler, influential in modern programming.
